CleverDesk Design

The CleverDeskproject aims to be the next generation of a library desk. This desk combines a motion detector and used it to monitor if anyone is using the desk. The Clever Desk works combined with an enormously customized app. It also connects to the school system for monitoring and management. The Clever Desk will also allow students to know that the desk is occupied or vacant by a giant screen outside the facility or by this customized app. It contains identification systems, provides more information to the school for management, and protects the right of students who have reservations. For the users who occupied the desk but had to leave in between, the desk also provides them with various choices. Users can renew the use time by clicking one button on the app, and there will be some specific rules designed for the users to avoid unnecessary risks and more convenience for the users and management team.

CleverDesk Design

CleverDesk is a design project made by our group. It aims to solve for inaccurate vacancy rate in the library. The CleverDesk uses a motion detector to monitor if anyone is using the desk and will show available seats both on a screen outside the facility and on the customized app. During the design process, our group did research for advice and improvement, created different personas for different scenarios, made the storyboard, and built the 3D model for the desk.

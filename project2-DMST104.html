CleverDesk Design

CleverDesk is a group design project that aims to solve the inaccurate vacancy problem in the libraries. The product we designed is a smart desk that has a built-in motion detector that can detect if there are people using the desk. There is also a customized app linked with the desk so that the user can check available seats before they come to the libraries. In this way, we aim to save the problem that people take too much time looking for seats in the library.

To start with the project, our group first talked about the problem of the unexpected long time it takes to look for seats in the library. During the designing process, we came up with different personas and different scenarios and agreed that our CleverDesk can improve this situation. It does not need too much user interaction because of its smart system, so even the elders can check the availability on their devices easily before they plan to come to the library. We discussed the potential cost for building such desks, researched for different types of motion detectors, and eventually picked the one that we have on the 3D model. We also gathered some user interests and suggestions through surveys for the CleverDesk. With all the information we collected, we eventually built the 3D model of the CleverDesk.
